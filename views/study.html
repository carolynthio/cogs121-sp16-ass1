STUDY LOCATIONS

Geisel Library


Description: Located in the middle of campus, Geisel seats up to 3000 people and has an Overnight Study Commons. More details about the different parts of Geisel can be found here.

Hours:

Monday-Thursday: 7:30 a.m. - 12:00 a.m.

Friday: 7:30 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.

Saturday: 10:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.

Sunday: 10:00 a.m. - 12:00 a.m.


Biomedical Library


Description: The Biomedical Library, also known as BML, is on the medical school campus, conveniently located near the Gilman/Meyers bus stop, and seats up to 610 people.

Hours:

Monday-Thursday: 8:00 a.m. - 12:00 a.m.

Friday: 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.

Saturday: 10:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.

Sunday: 10:00 a.m. - 12:00 a.m.


Price Center East


Description: Price Center East has a computer lab, as well as group study rooms that can be reserved, and various lounges for studying, and it seats up to 854.

Hours:

Monday-Friday: 24 hours a day

Saturday: 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.

Sunday: 9:30 a.m. - 12:00 a.m.


Price Center West


Description: Price Center West has a computer lab and also includes Sun God Lounge, seating up to 703 people total.

Hours:

Monday-Friday: 7:00 a.m. - 11:00 p.m.

Friday: 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m.

Saturday: 8:00 a.m. - 11:00 p.m.

Sunday: 9:30 a.m. - 11:00 p.m.


Galbraith Hall


Description: Galbraith Hall has both a collaborative lounge and a quiet study lounge, and it seats up to 130 people.

Hours:

Sunday-Saturday: 24 hours a day
